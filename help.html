What is this? Wizards... against the world of math. They walk through the trechorous world of Arithmatic, through the depths of Linear Algebra, collide with Topological Spaces. And then, right after it all seemed managable, an unexpected hypothesis comes along. Will the wizards make it out alive?! Controls: - Arrow keys or Gamepad 1 - movement for Player 1 - WASD keys or Gamepad 2 - movement for Player 2 - Q & P - Swap weapon for Player 1 & Player 2 - Enter & Space - Swap weapon for Player 1 & Player 2 The game explained? These math wizards have a way with numbers. You have to pickup the right operations to default the pieces of math.
